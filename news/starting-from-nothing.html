Design note

Why start from nothing?

Core fantasy

The central fantasy of From Scratch is not being handed a kingdom. It is the pressure of having no tools, no home, no security, and no stable social order.

That starting point makes every solved problem meaningful. A campfire, a crude weapon, a roof, a trusted ally, and a functioning settlement all become steps in a long chain of survival.

The goal is for progress to feel earned rather than decorative.
