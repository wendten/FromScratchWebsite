The game

Survive, organize, and expand.

From Scratch combines settlement management with grounded survival and character-driven stories. Your people need food, tools, shelter, protection, and leadership.

The world is intended to react to your decisions. A small camp can become a faction. A mistake can become a crisis. A weak survivor can become essential.

Settlement building

Gather materials, construct shelters, expand production, and turn a desperate camp into a functioning society.

Character simulation

NPCs have needs, skills, personalities, relationships, and memories that shape how they behave.

Combat and injury

Combat is designed around risk, stamina, wounds, equipment, and consequences rather than disposable health bars.

Crafting from materials

Weapons, tools, and structures are affected by the materials used to create them.

The world

A hostile vertical forest surrounded by mountains.

Huge trees, distant factions, scarce resources, and strange civilizations define the setting. The goal is a believable world where survival, culture, technology, and power all matter.
